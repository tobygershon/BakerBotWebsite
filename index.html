How It Works

1

Paste a recipe URL

Copy a link from any recipe website and paste it into Baker Bot. The app extracts the ingredients and steps in seconds.

2

Start voice mode

Tap "Start Voice" and Baker Bot will guide you through the recipe step by step. Switch to hands-free so you never have to touch your phone.

3

Cook and ask questions

Navigate with commands like "next step" or "previous ingredient." Ask questions, request substitutions, or have Baker Bot modify the recipe on the fly.

4

Save to your recipe book

Love it? Save the recipe (or your modified version) to your personal recipe book. Organize with folders and access from any device.
