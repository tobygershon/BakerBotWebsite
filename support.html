Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?

On iOS: Go to Settings > your name > Subscriptions > Baker Bot > Cancel Subscription. On Android: Open the Google Play Store > Menu > Subscriptions > Baker Bot > Cancel. Your access continues until the end of your current billing period.

How does the free trial work?

New Premium subscribers get a 1-week free trial. You won't be charged until the trial ends. If you cancel before the trial period is over, you won't be charged at all.

What's the difference between Standard and Premium?

Standard uses your device's built-in text-to-speech voice to read recipes. Premium uses a natural AI voice powered by OpenAI, which sounds much more human-like and conversational.

How do I use hands-free mode?

On the Voice screen, toggle "Hands-Free" on. Baker Bot will continuously listen for your commands without you needing to tap the record button. Say things like "next step," "next ingredient," or ask any cooking question.

Can I use Baker Bot without signing in?

Yes. You can extract and cook recipes without signing in. However, signing in with Google lets you save recipes to your recipe book and sync them across devices.

A recipe didn't extract correctly. What should I do?

Some websites use non-standard formatting that makes extraction difficult. Try a different recipe URL, or contact us with the URL and we'll look into improving support for that site.

How do I restore my purchases on a new device?

On the subscription screen, tap "Restore Purchases." This will restore any active subscriptions linked to your Apple ID or Google account.

How do I delete my data?

Local data is deleted when you uninstall the app. To delete your cloud-stored recipes and account data, please email us at the address above and we'll process your request.
